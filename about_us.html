Susanto Liman – Founder & CEO

Susanto Liman, the visionary behind MR.COFFEE, has over 25 years of experience in the coffee industry. With an unwavering passion for creating the perfect brew and a belief that every cup brings people together, he transformed a humble coffee cart into a cherished community haven. His journey is defined by creativity, resilience, and the art of connection—values that continue to inspire every cup served at MR.COFFEE.

Our Mission

At MR.COFFEE, our mission is to make quality coffee accessible every day. We are dedicated to delivering exceptional flavors, nurturing community connections, and crafting memorable experiences with every cup we serve.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a global symbol of warmth, creativity, and community. MR.COFFEE aspires to bridge cultures and connect people through a shared love for coffee, transforming every visit into an inspiring experience.

Our Story

In the heart of a bustling city, Mr.Coffee began as a small coffee shop with a big dream. With its quirky charm, mismatched chairs, and walls adorned with customer doodles, it quickly became a cozy refuge for dreamers, creatives, and coffee enthusiasts. Over the years, every cup served has carried stories of connection, laughter, and inspiration. Today, we invite you to be part of our journey as we continue to spread the magic of Mr.Coffee beyond its cozy walls.
